About!

The Typing Test consists of randomising quotes that you must type over as quick as you can. Measured in Words Per Minute (WPM). This is calculated by Keystrokes (number of characters / 5) / Time Taken. This will be your Raw Score but the official recording will be affected by your accuracy!
